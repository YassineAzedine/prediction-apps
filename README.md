# Prediction Apps Suite

This repository contains **3 Python Streamlit applications** for prediction tasks using **NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib**, and simple **Linear Regression** (Normal Equation).

---

## 1️⃣ House Price Prediction App
Predict the price of a house based on its features.

- **Features:**
  - Area (m²)
  - Number of Rooms
  - Age of building (years)
- **Interactive Graph:** Area vs Price
- **Output:** Price in USD ($)
- **Live Demo:** [Open in Streamlit](https://prediction-apps-afhzgx44qmq2xdmrutxbm4.streamlit.app/)

---

## 2️⃣ Car Price Prediction App
Predict the price of a car based on its characteristics.

- **Features:**
  - Year of manufacture
  - Brand
  - Mileage
  - Fuel type
  - Transmission
  - Engine size
- **Interactive Graph:** Mileage vs Price
- **Output:** Price in USD ($)
- **Live Demo:** [Open in Streamlit](https://prediction-apps-yzfqdyusx8ppacampfrdqe.streamlit.app/)

---

## 3️⃣ Employee Salary Prediction App
Predict the salary of an employee based on experience and other factors.

- **Features:**
  - Experience (years)
  - Education level (Bachelor, Master, PhD)
  - Department (IT, HR, Finance, Marketing)
  - Age
- **Interactive Graph:** Experience vs Salary
- **Output:** Salary in USD ($)
- **Live Demo:** [Open in Streamlit](https://prediction-apps-m9jisqsuwuevhramlhdwhs.streamlit.app/)
